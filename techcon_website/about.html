History

TechCon began in 2012 as a small developer meetup in Austin, Texas, aiming to create a more inclusive, hands-on tech event. It grew rapidly, introducing hackathons and attracting global tech leaders. By 2020, it successfully transitioned to a virtual format, reaching over 20,000 developers worldwide. Today, it’s a leading global tech conference focused on innovation, open-source, and ethical tech.

Mission

  • Innovation First - Championing cutting-edge technologies and creative problem-solving.
  • Open Access - Promoting open-source collaboration and knowledge sharing.
  • Diversity & Inclusion - Creating a welcoming space for all voices in tech.
